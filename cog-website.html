A / SECURITY MODEL

The "Household Tree"

  • Direct ownership. The head of household is the sole verified gatekeeper, ensuring no unverified third party can grant access.
  • Delegated authority. Residents invite co-occupants into their tree, with every code linked back to the primary account for full auditability.
  • Code integrity. Every QR or PIN entry is time-bound and single-use, ending contractor re-entry and code sharing.
B / UTILITY & COMMUNITY

The Resident Hub

  • Automated utilities. Buy electricity and water tokens directly in-app with instant delivery and usage analytics.
  • The estate feed. A dedicated channel for real-time updates, security alerts and community engagement.
  • Triple-threat SOS. One high-visibility button for fire, medical and security emergencies.
C / GATE GOVERNANCE

The Guard Portal

  • Digital pass/fail. Replaces unreliable logbooks with a streamlined guard verification tool.
  • Instant verification. Guards validate entry in seconds, keeping gate flow smooth without compromising security.
  • The audit foundation. Every check-in is auto-logged with a timestamp for forensic security review.
ZERO-HARDWARE

MUSA runs on the smartphones residents and guards already own and deploys entirely on cloud infrastructure, delivering elite-level governance without the capital burden of proprietary gate hardware.
