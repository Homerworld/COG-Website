PILLAR A

Infrastructure & Real Estate Operations

We run buildings the way a quantity surveyor reads a cost plan — with discipline, foresight and a paper trail. From handover to long-term lifecycle planning, COG keeps residential and commercial assets stable, compliant and financially predictable.

Overview

What this pillar covers

COG’s facility practice is built on a simple economics: maintenance is cheaper than repair, and repair is cheaper than replacement. We structure operations around preventive schedules, condition data and clear governance, so owners and estate boards always know the state — and the cost — of their assets.

Where most operators react to failures, we plan against them. Every property under our care runs on a maintained asset register and a planned-maintenance calendar, with reporting that feeds directly into the MUSA platform for live visibility.
