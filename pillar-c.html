PILLAR C

Contracting, Procurement & Global Logistics

From the bill of quantities to the bonded warehouse, COG runs the full supply chain behind a project. We combine quantity surveying and cost-governance discipline with international sourcing and Nigerian port-clearing expertise to land the right materials, at the right cost, on time.

Overview

What this pillar covers

Large projects rarely fail on design — they fail on procurement, cost control and logistics. Our contracting practice is led by quantity surveyors, so cost governance is built in from the bill of quantities onward, not bolted on after overruns appear.

Because we manage sourcing and freight end-to-end, the chain from international supplier to site stays accountable: one team, one cost trail, from purchase order to last-mile delivery.
